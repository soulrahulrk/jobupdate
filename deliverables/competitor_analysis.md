**Your edge today:** a *curated, fresher-first, AI/ML-focused* board with AI resume review + job-match — a clear niche the giants don't serve well. The gaps below are about depth, not direction.

## 2. Missing — candidate tools
- **Job alerts + saved searches** (biggest retention lever the giants rely on).
- **Email digests** ("12 new AI fresher roles in NCR this week").
- **Application autofill** from stored resume/profile.
- **Salary insights / company reviews** (Glassdoor's moat).
- **Referral/ask-for-referral** flow (Wellfound/LinkedIn).
- **Skill-gap report** ("you match 7/10; learn X") — natural extension of your AI match.

## 3. Missing — recruiter/employer tools
- **Self-serve "Post a Job"** + company claim/verify.
- **Applicant pipeline** (Application model exists → just needs an employer view + stages).
- **Candidate search** by skills/experience/location.
- **Bulk import** (CSV / ATS sync) — you already have ATS ingestion plumbing.
- **Analytics for employers** (views, apply rate, time-to-fill).

## 4. Missing — AI features (where you can leapfrog)
- **AI search** ("remote LLM jobs for 2026 grads under ₹12L") → natural-language → filters.
- **AI cover-letter / application tailoring** from JD + resume.
- **AI interview-prep** per job (DSA + role-specific).
- **AI JD generator** for recruiters.
- **Semantic job recommendations** (embeddings + your ChromaDB-style stack) instead of keyword `contains`.
- **Auto skill-tagging** of jobs/companies (normalize the arrays).

## 5. Positioning recommendation
Don't out-breadth Naukri. **Win the "AI/ML & tech fresher in India" wedge** with: (1) the best AI match + resume tooling, (2) curated *verified* roles (your `hiringStatus`/`source` discipline), (3) SEO facet pages for `<role> + <city>` long-tail, (4) job alerts for retention. Then expand to a light **recruiter self-serve** to monetize.
