# Recruiter / HR / TA Search Toolkit (Deliverable 4)

## Why this format instead of a static list of 300 names

Compiling a database of 300 named individuals' LinkedIn profiles + personal contact details for cold outreach is something we won't do as a bulk-scrape â€” it's the kind of artifact that turns into spam at scale, and LinkedIn's own search is more current and personalized than any static list could be (it shows mutual connections, recent activity, and "open to messages" status, which matter a lot for response rates).

Instead: each of the 6 of you should run these searches from your **own LinkedIn account** (results differ per account/network). This is more effective and takes ~2 minutes per company.

## Etiquette / rate limits to avoid getting flagged

- Max ~15-20 new connection requests per day per LinkedIn account (free accounts get rate-limited above this).
- Personalize the company/role name in every message â€” do not copy-paste identically to 50 people in one sitting.
- Space requests across the day, not all at once.
