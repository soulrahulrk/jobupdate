# Paid Training & "Placement Guarantee" Programs — Honest Assessment

You asked about options that charge a fee, since speed matters. Here's the real landscape, found via search today, plus a framework so the team doesn't lose money to a bad one.

## The core rule first

**Real recruitment agencies and real employers never charge candidates a fee** — they're paid by the hiring company. Anything calling itself a "recruitment agency" or "placement consultancy" that asks *you* for money before/for a job offer is not operating like a normal agency, full stop.

What's *legitimately* fee-based in this market is **training-with-placement-assistance** — you pay for a skills course (testing, MERN/MEAN stack, cloud, etc.), and the institute helps you apply afterward. This can be worth it **if**:
- The fee is for actual instruction/curriculum (verifiable syllabus, instructors, duration), not a vague "registration fee."
- "Placement assistance" is explicitly NOT a guaranteed job — read the fine print. "100% placement guarantee" claims should be treated with skepticism; ask for **proof**: names of recent batches, LinkedIn profiles of people who got placed, which companies hired them and when.
- You can verify the institute has a real office (visit it) and has been operating for years (check Google reviews, Glassdoor, MCA/company registration via zaubacorp.com).

## Before paying anyone, anything — checklist

1. **Get the fee structure in writing** (what exactly does it cover, refund policy if no placement).
2. **Ask for 3-5 recent alumni** you can actually message on LinkedIn — message them yourself, don't just trust testimonials on the institute's site.
3. **Check company registration** on zaubacorp.com or mca.gov.in — confirms it's a real registered entity, not a fly-by-night operation.
4. **Visit the physical office** before paying — addresses above are a starting point.
5. **Never pay via UPI/cash to an individual's personal account** — payment should go to the registered company's bank account/official payment gateway, with a proper invoice/receipt.
6. **Compare the fee to the 30-day free plan** in [08_30day_execution_plan.md](08_30day_execution_plan.md) — six of you doing the free plan consistently for 4-6 weeks may outperform a paid course, especially since you already have B.Tech CSE (AI/ML) fundamentals. Paid training makes most sense for a *specific skill gap* (e.g., one of you needs Power BI or a cloud cert), not as a blanket "guaranteed job" purchase.
