# Walk-in Visit Plan (Deliverable 5)

## Important caveats before you go

1. **"Walk-in Allowed" was not confirmed for any agency/company below** — none of the sources found explicitly advertise open walk-ins for fresher CVs. **Call ahead** (numbers in [01_recruitment_agencies.csv](01_recruitment_agencies.csv) / [02_companies.csv](02_companies.csv)) to confirm someone in HR/recruitment will actually meet you — otherwise you risk a wasted trip to a reception desk that just takes a CV and does nothing with it.
2. **Travel times/distances below are rough estimates from general Tricity geography**, not a live Maps query (I don't have live Maps access right now). Before leaving, paste the addresses into Google Maps yourself for the actual route — sequence is more reliable than my time estimates.
3. Carry 8-10 printed resumes + digital copies on phone, dress formally, go in pairs/small groups (not all 6 at once to the same small office — it can overwhelm a 5-person HR team and hurts impression).
