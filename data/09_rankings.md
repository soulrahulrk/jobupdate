# Rankings (Deliverable 9)

**Scope note:** Rankings below are drawn from the verified entries in [01_recruitment_agencies.csv](01_recruitment_agencies.csv) and [02_companies.csv](02_companies.csv) — the real, sourced datasets built across 2026-06-11 and 2026-06-14. **These rankings cover the Chandigarh/Tricity entries only.** As of 2026-06-14 the CSVs also include a Delhi-NCR section (8 NCR agencies + 20 NCR companies, mostly large national employers like TCS/Infosys/Adobe/HCLTech/Genpact); those are not ranked here because NCR is a different, far larger market that deserves its own ranking pass — for NCR, use the interactive [Hiring_Dashboard.html](../Hiring_Dashboard.html) (filter by the NCR tab) which already sorts by fresher-fit and AI/ML-fit. The original ask was for Top 25/50/50/50 from pools of 100/200/300; those pools don't exist as verified data yet (see [README.md](../README.md) for why). These rankings are honest given current data size — re-run once the lists are expanded.

Ranking criteria (scored qualitatively 1-5 each, summed): **Fresher friendliness, AI/ML relevance, hiring activity signal, growth/stability, accessibility (walk-in/local presence)**.
