# 30-Day Execution Plan for 6 B.Tech CSE (AI/ML) Freshers — Tricity Job Search

Start date: assume Day 1 = next Monday after kickoff. Plan runs Mon-Sat each week (Sunday = rest/buffer/catch-up).

## Team setup (Day 0, before Day 1)

- Split the 6 into pairs for accountability: Pair A, Pair B, Pair C.
- Create a shared tracker (Google Sheet) with columns: Date | Person | Action Type (Application/Outreach/Walk-in/Networking) | Company | Role | Link | Status | Follow-up Date.
- Each person sets up: a polished resume (1 page), an updated LinkedIn profile, a GitHub with 2-3 pinned ML/SDE projects, and (optional but strong) a portfolio site (GitHub Pages/Vercel).

---

**Goal:** Get application materials production-ready and start volume applications immediately (don't wait for "perfect" resume).

|---|---|---|---|

**Week 1 KPIs:** 47 applications/person (282 team total), 25 recruiter connections/person, 1 walk-in completed, resumes finalized, GitHub live.

## Days 25-30: Buffer, review, and reset

- Day 25-26: Team retrospective — review the tracker. Which channels (agencies vs. direct applications vs. LinkedIn outreach vs. walk-ins) produced the most interview calls? Double down on what's working.
- Day 27-28: Refresh resumes/LinkedIn based on interview feedback received so far.
- Day 29: Plan Month 2 — likely needs a 2nd pass through agencies that didn't respond, plus newly opened roles.
- Day 30: Celebrate progress regardless of outcome — job searches in this market commonly take 6-10 weeks for freshers; consistency over 30 days builds the pipeline that converts in months 2-3.
